About me:

I'm a Minneapolis based Software Engineer and IT consultant. I work for Optum Technology's Innovation and R&D team otherwise known as The Garage. I build MVP technology projects following the Lean model. Most of my work there is done in Ruby on Rails and Postgres, but I'm also pursuing some Raspberry Pi and Ardunio IoT projects. The Garage itself operates like a startup incubator running inside UnitedHealth Group.

Before Optum I worked for CaringBridge, a nonprofit offering a protected place for people experiencing a health journey to share and experience love, hope, and compassion. CaringBridge is a Zend Framework 2 app, and most of my work there was in PHP and MongoDB. My previous experience involved building a PPACA compliance product for a health insurance consulting company. Before that I was a double major in Physics and Philosophy at Hamline University in St. Paul, Minnesota.
